Family Series - Begins this Sunday!

From the siblings who are best friends, to the parents with distant kids, and the uncles who are more than a little crazy, we all have family. But no matter what your family history is, there is an even deeper legacy we all have access to as believers. Throughout the Family Series, we will dive into learning about God's best for us in different stages of family life and what a Godly family looks like. Invite your people and meet us there!
 

VBS Volunteer Interest Meeting

VBS is an intentional time for kids to hear the gospel, learn Scripture, have fun, and tell their friends about Jesus. VBS will be June 14-16, and while we have a team working to make it the best experience for our kids, we can't do it without our amazing volunteers!

 

If you are interested in serving at VBS and would like to know more about it, you're invited to join us for our VBS Volunteer Interest Meeting this Sunday, April 24 at 1:00pm in the Chapel. To sign up to serve, text VBS to 88000!
 

Collide Camp

Collide Camp is next weekend! From April 29-May 1, our 8-12th grade students will gather from across the state to encounter God, come into or back into a relationship with Jesus, and be empowered to use what God has given them to change their schools, family, and the world around them. Collide Camp will take place at Springlake Baptist Camp in Lonsdale, AR and currently costs $70. The price increases this Sunday, April 24, so be sure to head over to nlccollide.tv to sign your student up for a weekend they won't forget!
